Who we are

Security for the
real world

Most cybersecurity consultancy is built for large enterprises with unlimited budgets and dedicated IT departments. Small businesses and individuals are often left with overcomplicated tools they don't need, or jargon-filled reports they can't act on.

At Soroflow we do things differently. We believe security should be a foundation for growth — not a barrier. We combine technical expertise with a background in business law and finance to deliver solutions that are legally sound, economically sensible and practically achievable.

We work with small and medium businesses and individuals across Denmark and the EU.

  • No hidden costs — fixed scope, fixed price
