Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Soroflow uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (soroflow.com). We only set non-essential cookies (such as analytics) after you give explicit consent via the cookie banner. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time by clearing the cookieConsent entry from your browser's site data.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies can be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (stored for a set period of time).

2. Cookies we use

Our website does not set any first-party cookies. However, the following third-party services used on our website may set cookies:

3. How to control cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website.
